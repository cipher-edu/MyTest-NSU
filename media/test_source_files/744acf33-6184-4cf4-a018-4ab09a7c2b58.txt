'''Muxiddin Baxriddinovich Kalonov''' ({{langx|uz|Muxiddin Baxriddinovich Kalonov}}; {{langx|ru|Мухиддин Бахриддинович Калонов}}; born 11 July 1977) is an Uzbek economist, academic, and university administrator. He has served as the rector of Navoiy State Pedagogical University since June 2024.<ref>{{cite web |title=Navoiy State Pedagogical Institute Appoints New Rector |url=https://daryo.uz/2024/06/24/rektor-almashgani |website=Daryo.uz |publisher=Daryo |date=24 June 2024 |access-date=16 May 2025 |language=uz}}</ref> Kalonov is a Doctor of Economic Sciences, professor, member of the [[Writers' Union of Uzbekistan]], and a permanent consultant and expert for the Senate of the [[Oliy Majlis]] and the Anti-Corruption Agency of Uzbekistan. He is also a member of the [[Ecological Party of Uzbekistan]] and a deputy in the Navoiy City Council.

== Early life and education ==
Muxiddin Kalonov was born in 1977 in [[Nurota District]], [[Navoiy Region]], into a family of intellectuals. He graduated with honors from the [[Tashkent Institute of Irrigation and Agricultural Mechanization Engineers]] (TIIAME) in 2000. During his studies, he worked as a proofreader for the academic journal ''Iqtisod va Hisobot'' (1996–1997) and as a technical editor for ''PressTIJ'' (1997–1998). He later served as chief accountant for "Alp-Farzin" (1998–1999) and the editorial office of ''Fidokor'' newspaper (1999–2002).

Kalonov completed advanced studies at the [[Academy of State and Social Construction under the President of Uzbekistan]], the Higher Business School, and the Center for Corporate Management in 2006. In 2012, he earned a master's degree in accounting and foreign economic activity analysis from the [[Tashkent State University of Economics]] (TSUE). He pursued further training at the Higher School of Business and Entrepreneurship in 2021 and completed postdoctoral research at the [[University of Oxford]] in 2023.

As an independent researcher at TSUE, Kalonov specialized in accounting, economic analysis, and auditing in transport logistics. He undertook academic internships at [[Peking University]] (QS Top 12) in China and [[Saint Petersburg State University of Economics]] (BRICS Top 150) in Russia. He earned a PhD in Economics in 2017 and a Doctor of Economic Sciences (DSc) degree in 2019.<ref name="oak">{{cite web |title=Announcement of Muxiddin Baxriddinovich Kalonov's Doctoral (DSc) Dissertation Defense |url=https://oak.uz/pages/3315 |website=OAK |publisher=Higher Attestation Commission under the Ministry of Higher Education, Science, and Innovations of Uzbekistan |access-date=16 May 2025 |language=uz}}</ref>

== Academic and professional career ==
From 2002 to 2018, Kalonov worked at the "Avtodaryotrans-ilm" State Scientific-Methodological Center, progressing from researcher to deputy director for economic and financial affairs, and later to general director. Between 2018 and 2020, he served as general director of the Institute for Advanced Training and Retraining of Automotive Transport Personnel under Uzbekistan’s Ministry of Transport. Concurrently, he was a professor in the Accounting Department, head of the Corporate Management and Logistics Department, and director of the Research Center for "Scientific Foundations and Issues of Uzbekistan’s Economic Development" at TSUE. From 2020 to 2024, he served as TSUE’s vice-rector. In June 2024, he was appointed rector of Navoiy State Pedagogical University.

In 2019, Kalonov presented a digitization and scientific-technical project for the modernized Tashkent bus terminal at the government level. He is the founder and editor-in-chief of the academic journal ''Logistics and Economics'' and a member of the editorial boards of several journals recognized by Uzbekistan’s Higher Attestation Commission (OAK). In 2018, he pioneered a distance learning platform for professional training in Uzbekistan. He authored the book ''Organization of Accounting in Automotive Transport Enterprises'' and founded the "Think Tank" club at TSUE’s Research Center, focusing on analytical research, strategic planning, economic technologies, econometric modeling, and regional economic analysis.

Since 2022, Kalonov has led the international project "Ready4Trade Central Asia" and overseen one fundamental project (on international financial reporting standards in Uzbekistan), two applied projects, and one innovative project. In 2023, he established a "Regional Laboratory" at [[Urgench State University]].

== Research and publications ==
Kalonov has authored over 200 academic works on transport logistics, accounting, economic analysis, and auditing. He has published two monographs and more than 30 textbooks and study guides, some of which have appeared in international publications. His works include ''Introduction to Taxation and Accounting'', ''Accounting'', ''Accounting and Auditing'', ''Financial Accounting'', and ''Management Accounting''.
